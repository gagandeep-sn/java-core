# Calculate Commission Percentage

## Category

Miscellaneous

## Difficulty

Easy

## Concepts

* Input Output
* Basic Operations

## Code Logic

1. The program first prompts the user to enter the sales amount and stores it in the variable 'Sale'.
2. It then prompts the user to enter the commission earned and stores it in the variable 'Comission'.
3. The commission percentage is calculated by dividing the commission by the sales amount and multiplying by 100, and the result is stored in the variable 'comper'.
4. Finally, the program prints out the calculated commission percentage.

## Time Complexity

O(1)

## Space Complexity

O(1)

## Interview Relevance

Low

Beginner

## Explanation

This program calculates the commission percentage earned from a given sales amount. It takes two inputs from the user: the sales amount and the commission earned. It then calculates the commission percentage by dividing the commission by the sales amount and multiplying by 100. The final output is the commission percentage.
